BUBS, or B* Under Berks Station, is an attempt at having an early-morning breakfast spot open before you have to be at your desk in Center City (so, Breakfast Under Berks Station) and also a sober-friendly third space open late for, say, playing games with friends (so, Boardgames Under Berks Station). More about our philosophy in a minute, but first, the stuff you're probably here for:

Info
  • LOCATION: 102 W Berks St, Retail 1
  • HOURS: 2-10pm Monday-Friday
  • PRICES: $30 per hour per table, or $18 for a half table
  • RENTAL: $150/hr for the whole space or book us via PeerSpace.com
  • CONTACT: bubsphl@gmail.com
On Tap
  • ON FOOD: Right now BUBS does not have its food license, so we're not open for breakfast. We DO want you to come in and use our space, so please BYO - we love Olive Branch, Mr Pan's, Pasha's, and all the food trucks that set up in front of Sacred Vice.
  • ON TABLES: Our pricing is per table because we want you to bring in a big group and get that cost per hour down to a minimum.
  • ON EVERYTHING ELSE: This is a work in progress. Edits and maybe something slightly more professional to come.
